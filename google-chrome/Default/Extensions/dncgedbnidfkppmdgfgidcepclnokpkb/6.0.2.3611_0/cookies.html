What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data that are stored on your computer or other device when mobile applications load on your device or when websites are loaded in a browser, including websites that provide online software. Cookies are used to recognize your device and to store your preferences. Cookies may store your preferences for a single visit or for multiple repeat visits. Some cookies transmit data back to Vernier or third party hosts, while others are local storage items that do not transmit any data. Cookies may be set by Vernier or by third parties to serve content or provide advertising or analytics services on to Vernier

You can opt out of accepting cookies by adjusting your browser settings to not accept cookies or to notify you when you are sent a cookie.
